History and Culture

Punjab, the land of five rivers, has been the cradle of ancient civilizations and a melting pot of diverse cultures. The region has witnessed the rise and fall of numerous empires and dynasties, each leaving its distinct mark on Punjab's rich cultural tapestry.

Geography

Punjab is Pakistan's second-largest province by area, located in the country's east. It shares borders with Indian Punjab to the east, Sindh to the south, Balochistan to the southwest, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa to the west, and Islamabad Capital Territory to the north.

Economy

Punjab is Pakistan's most industrialized province and often referred to as the breadbasket of the country due to its extensive agricultural production. The province contributes significantly to Pakistan's textile industry, and its cities like Lahore, Faisalabad, and Sialkot are major industrial centers.
