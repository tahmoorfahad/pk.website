Major Cities of Pakistan

Explore the vibrant urban centers of our nation

Karachi

The largest city and economic hub of Pakistan, known for its diverse culture and bustling port.

  • Population: 14.9 million
  • Province: Sindh
  • Known for: Port city, Financial center

Lahore

The cultural heart of Pakistan, famous for its historical architecture and rich Mughal heritage.

  • Population: 11.1 million
  • Province: Punjab
  • Known for: Historical monuments, Food

Islamabad

The capital city, known for its modern architecture and beautiful natural surroundings.

  • Population: 1.2 million
  • Capital Territory
  • Known for: Government offices, Modern planning

Peshawar

The oldest living city in South Asia, known for its rich history and traditional bazaars.

  • Population: 2 million
  • Province: KPK
  • Known for: Historical bazaars, Culture

Historical Cities

Multan

City of Saints, known for its Sufi shrines and ancient history

Thatta

Former capital of Sindh, famous for its necropolis

Taxila

Ancient Buddhist learning center and archaeological site
